Welcome to CivicConnect

CivicConnect is your gateway to reporting and tracking community issues. Whether it's a broken water pipe, unlit street, or sanitation problem—this platform helps connect you with the right agency.

1. Submit a Complaint

Fill out a simple form including your name, email, complaint category, description, and location. Optionally upload a photo to help illustrate the issue.

2. Automatic Assignment

Your complaint is automatically sent to the appropriate government agency based on the category you choose (Water, Roads, Electricity, etc).

3. Track Your Complaint

Use your email and category to check the status of your complaint. View updates, responses, or resolutions made by the agency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CivicConnect free?
Yes. CivicConnect is completely free for citizens to report issues and track them.

Do I need to create an account?
No. You only need your email and complaint category to track your issue.

What if I don’t see my complaint in the dashboard?
Make sure you filled the form completely. If needed, resubmit or contact your local agency.

Who can respond to my complaint?
Only verified government agencies or admins can respond and update your complaint status.
