About CivicConnect

CivicConnect is an innovative government platform designed to help citizens report issues and raise concerns directly to the relevant agencies. Our mission is to build a transparent, responsive, and accountable public service.

Our Services

  • Online complaint submission for different categories (e.g., waste, roads, corruption)
  • Automatic complaint assignment to the responsible agency
  • Real-time complaint status updates
  • Communication and response system between citizens and government departments

Our Vision

To empower every citizen with a voice, and every government agency with the tools to respond effectively and efficiently.
